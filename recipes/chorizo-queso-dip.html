Chorizo Queso Dip

Ingredients

  • 10oz Bulk Chorizo Sausage
  • One 10oz Can Diced tomatoes with chili peppers - Drained
  • One 8oz package cream cheese - cubed
  • One 8oz processed cheese (Veleeta) - cubed

Directions

Step One

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir chorizo in the hot skillet until well browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain well and transfer to a slow cooker.

Step Two

Add diced tomatoes with chiles, cream cheese, and processed cheese to slow cooker; stir well. Cover and cook on Low until cheese has melted, stirring occasionally, about 1 Â½ to 2 hours.
